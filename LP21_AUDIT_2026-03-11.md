**Root Cause:** Difficulty-3 exercises across Grades 1–3 are systematically written at Gymnasium (Grade 7–10) or university level rather than at grade-level enrichment. ## LP21 Reference Standards

### Cycle 1 (Grades 1–2) — MA.1 Zahlen und Variablen
- Grade 1: Numbers 0–20, basic +/−, simple repeating patterns
- Grade 2: Numbers to 100, +/− within 20 (then 100), even/odd, introduction to multiplication as repeated addition (late Grade 2)
- **NOT in Cycle 1:** Multiplication tables, division, fractions, sequences, prime numbers, symmetry axes, decimal arithmetic

### Cycle 1 (Grades 1–2) — DE Deutsch
- Letter recognition, phonics, simple sentences
- Basic verb forms (Präsens, Perfekt introduced mid-Cycle 1)
- **NOT in Cycle 1:** Konjunktiv, Passiv, Hypotaxe, literary devices, complex text types

### Cycle 2 (Grades 3–6) — MA.1
- Grade 3: Numbers to 1000, multiplication tables (1×1), simple division, ½ and ¼ fractions
- Grade 4: Numbers to 10,000, written multiplication/division, decimal intro, fractions with common denominators
- Grade 5: Numbers to 1,000,000, long division, percentages intro, negative numbers
- Grade 6: Large numbers, decimals, ratios, proportions, simple equations

### Cycle 2 (Grades 3–4) — DE Deutsch (LP21)
- Grade 3: Sentence structure (Subjekt/Prädikat/Objekt), noun/verb/adjective, Perfekt/Präteritum, simple text comprehension, letter writing
- Grade 4: Compound sentences, Plusquamperfekt intro, synonyms, text comprehension with inference
- **NOT in Grades 3–4:** Konjunktiv I/II, Passiv Perfekt, Futur II, Hypotaxe, Anakoluth, Gerundiv, Metonymie, Haiku analysis, Episches Theater, Novelle vs. Kurzgeschichte

### Cycle 2 (Grades 3–4) — NMG (Natur, Mensch, Gesellschaft)
- Grade 3: Basic ecosystems, simple properties of materials, light/shadow basics, community and local geography, seasonal changes
- **NOT in Grades 3–4:** Isostasie, Cherenkov-Strahlung, Kernfusion, Supraleiter, Habeas Corpus, Geoengineering, Eutrophierung, Ohmsches Gesetz

## Systemic Recommendations

### 1. Difficulty-3 Content Policy
The difficulty-3 tier needs a clear definition: **"Challenging exercise still within the LP21 competency level for this grade, requiring problem-solving or multi-step thinking — NOT content from a higher cycle."**

Currently, difficulty-3 appears to mean "what would an expert ask about this topic at any level," which is incorrect.

### 2. Grade 3 German — Full Revision Required
All difficulty-3 exercises in Satzlehre, Verben, Synonyme, Textsorten, and Adjektive should be audited against LP21 DE.3.A (Sprache und Sprachgebrauch untersuchen). The confirmed examples (Konjunktiv, Anakoluth, Gerundiv, Metonymie) indicate systematic over-scoping.

### 3. Grade 3 Science — Full Revision Required
All difficulty-3 exercises in Energie, Licht, Erde, and Demokratie should be replaced. The confirmed examples (Cherenkov, Kernfusion, Supraleiter, Isostasie, Habeas Corpus) are not enrichment — they are entirely outside the LP21 NMG curriculum for any primary school grade.

### 4. Grade 1 Math — Patterns Topic Redesign
The `muster` topic's difficulty-3 exercises (Fibonacci, Quadratzahlen, exponential sequences) should be completely replaced. Grade 1 pattern enrichment should be: color/shape alternating patterns, +1/+2 number patterns within 20, simple ABAB sequences.

### 5. Grades 4–6 Spot-Check
Run a targeted audit on difficulty-3 exercises in Grades 4–6 to verify the pattern does not continue.
